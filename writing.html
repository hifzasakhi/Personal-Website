Writing

I have loved writing since I was younger. In middle school and high school, I was always the student who exceeded page limits on assignments and turned in long answers during exams. At Georgia Tech, I decided to pursue this passion to the fullest by joining several writing clubs. My first position started in the Pioneer, which is a newspaper in the Biomedical Engineering department. My articles have showcased a professor's minority initiative efforts, the BMES meeting, admission process for dental school, and biotechnology news. Through this club, I learned about the importance of teamwork and how the success of our publication depended on continuous communication and collaboration between different parties, including the photographers, writers, persons being interviewed for the content, article editors, layout editors, and the editor in chief.
